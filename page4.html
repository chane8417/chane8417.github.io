Trivia about the casting of Marley and Me

  1. Owen Wilson's real parents were casted as his parents in the movie. His mom kept accidentally calling him Owen.
  2. The author of the book "Marley and Me" was the person walking the cocker spaniel in the dog training class. (see video 4 in the video part)
  3. Three different kids were casted as Patrick Grogan.
  4. Two different kids were casted as Conor Grogan.
  5. A labrador named Clyde was almost as naughty as the Marley in the movie and had the most screen time out of all the labradors.
