About

Above is an image of the three main characters in Marley and Me. Marley and Me is a movie revolving around a labrador retriever named Marley. The movie follows Marley's life as he grows up under John and Jenny Grogan's care. This website is about this movie, with a summary about the movie, the movie's main characters, some video clips from the movie, and the actors that made this movie.
