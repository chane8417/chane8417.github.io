Marley

Marley is a yellow labrador retriever. He is one of the main characters of the movie. Marley was originally adopted from a shelter because John Grogan wanted to prepare him and Jennifer for a baby. Despite being the runt of the litter, Marley is very excitable and never runs out of energy, which causes him to get into a lot of trouble.
